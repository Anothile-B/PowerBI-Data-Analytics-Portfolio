# Project Overview
The portfolio includes two dashboards developed to analyze retail and e-commerce datasets. 
Each project focuses on solving business problems through data-driven insights and interactive visualizations.

---
# 1. Retail Sales Dashboard

# Problem Definition
Retail organizations often lack centralized visibility into sales performance across products, regions, and time periods. 
This limits their ability to identify trends, optimize inventory, and maximize profitability.

# Analytical Approach
- Performed data cleaning and transformation to ensure consistency and accuracy
- Structured data for analysis using categorical grouping and time-based fields
- Built interactive visualizations to enable dynamic filtering and drill-down analysis

# Key Metrics & Visualizations
- Total Sales and Profit KPIs
- Sales trends over time
- Sales distribution by product category
- Regional performance comparison

# Business Impact
- Enables identification of top-performing products and categories
- Supports data-driven inventory and sales planning
- Improves strategic decision-making through visual insights

---

# 2. E-Commerce Analytics Dashboard

# Problem Definition
E-commerce platforms generate high-volume transactional data but often lack structured insights into customer behavior,
product performance, and operational costs such as shipping.

# Analytical Approach
- Cleaned and prepared transactional data for analysis
- Segmented customers by age group and geographic location
- Analyzed product-level and category-level performance
- Incorporated cost analysis (shipping) into overall performance evaluation

# Key Metrics & Visualizations
- Total Sales KPI for overall performance tracking
- Sales by Product Category and Product Name
- Customer segmentation by Age Group
- Geographic sales distribution using map visualization
- Shipping Cost analysis

# Business Impact
- Identifies high-revenue products and categories
- Supports targeted marketing strategies based on customer demographics
- Enhances geographic decision-making using location-based insights
- Helps optimize operational costs such as shipping
